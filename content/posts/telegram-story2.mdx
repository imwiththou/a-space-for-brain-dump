A few months ago I've criticised the new story feature in Telegram and I thought it was created to compete with Instagram story in terms of getting active users and a share of the huge social media market. 

It turned out this feature became my favourite recently and I was wrong in my judgement on the story feature in Telegram. It might be a wonderful thing for me to enjoy what social media should be in this explosive information era.
